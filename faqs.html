Frequently Asked Questions

← Back to Conference Home

What is the conference schedule?

  • Friday: Sessions, workshops, and a panel discussion after lunch
  • Saturday: Workshops, Awards Luncheon, and the Business Meeting
  • Exhibitors: Available both days, with scheduled exploration times and raffles

What's the difference between sessions, workshops, and panels?

  • Workshops: In-depth, extended learning on a focused topic led by local and national speakers
    • Friday: Two 2-hour workshops
    • Saturday: Five 2-hour workshops
  • Sessions: 60-minute presentations (Friday only)
    • Friday includes over 30 sessions
  • Panels: Interactive, discussion-based presentations held after Friday lunch

What registration options are available?

  • Full Conference Registration (Friday + Saturday)
  • Saturday-Only Registration
  • Meals included vary by registration type; additional meals may be added
  • Business Meeting attendance does not require registration for the Awards Luncheon

What meals are provided and can dietary needs be accommodated?

  • Coffee and tea are available at designated times.
  • Friday lunch is included with registration.
  • Dietary options include gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan.

Where should I stay?

  • A limited discounted room block is available at the Wellsworth Hotel.
  • Rooms are first-come, first-served.

Is parking available?

  • On-site parking is available
  • MAFLA is not responsible for vehicles or personal property

Where do I check in when I arrive?

  • Location: First floor
  • You will receive:
    • Your conference badge
    • Tickets for any additional purchases
  • All sessions and workshops are located on the first floor
  • Refer to the Venue tab for details

What if I didn't register online or want to add something on-site?

  • Visit Member Services on the first floor
  • MAFLA volunteers can help you:
    • Register on-site
    • Add extras
    • Join or renew your MAFLA membership
  • Payment accepted: credit card, check, or cash

Is Wi-Fi available?

Yes. Wi-Fi is available to all attendees. Login information is posted on the conference website.

I'm presenting — what should I bring?

Please bring a dongle/adapter to connect your device to the projector or TV (HDMI).

Who organizes the conference?

The conference is supported by the MAFLA Board and coordinated by the Conference Chair and Coordinator.
